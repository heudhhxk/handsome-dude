IONS

An ion is an atom that carries a positive or negatve sign.Examples are H- Hitrogen ion,Cl2- calcium ion.Some atoms do no have a negative nor positive sign that means they are noble gases eg Ar which is Argon,Argon is a noble gas since it is the 18th element of the periodic table.If you want to calculate the ion of each element you need to know the four(4)shells which are the K shell,L shell,M shell and N shell.The K shell can only carry 2 electrons, the l shell can only carry 8 electrons,the M shell can only carry 8 for beginners while 18 in advanced classesand the N shell can only carry 36 shells.NOTE that the positive sign does not mean adding electron it means subtracting or giving out electrons while the negative sign does not mean subtrcting it means adding or bringing in an electron
